Syllabus Of GATE 2025

GATE Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Subjects Target 2025


The GATE Data Science and Artificial Intelligence subjects cover a range of topics that are important for understanding and excelling in the field. Some of the key GATE Data Science and Artificial Intelligence subjects included in the syllabus are:
•	General Aptitude
•	Probability and Statistics
•	Linear Algebra
•	Calculus and Optimization
•	Programming, Data Structures, and Algorithms
•	Database Management and Warehousing
•	Machine Learning
•	AI (Artificial Intelligence)
